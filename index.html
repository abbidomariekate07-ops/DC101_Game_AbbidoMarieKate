🎯 Game Description

Mole Mayhem is an exciting browser-based Whack-a-Mole game where players test their reflexes and hand-eye coordination. Click on the moles as they pop up from their holes to score points before time runs out!

🎮 How to Play

  • Click the "Start Game" button to begin
  • Moles will randomly pop up from holes
  • Click on the moles as fast as you can
  • Each successful hit gives you 10 points
  • You have 30 seconds to score as high as possible
  • Try to beat your high score!

✨ Game Features

  • ⏱️ 30-second countdown timer
  • 🏆 Score tracking system
  • 💾 Persistent high score storage
  • 🎨 Smooth animations and transitions
  • 📱 Fully responsive design
  • 🎉 Performance feedback on game over
